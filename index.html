About Me

Hi! My name is Emily. I'm a current student at UCLA Extension, and am learning how to code. I live in Los Angeles, California. I am originally from Ann Arbor, Michigan (where my immediate family still lives.) After high school, I went to Western Michigan University in Kalamazoo to study fashion design.

During my Junior year, I studied in London through a study abroad program. During that time, my mom had a health scare, so I decided to transfer to the University of Michigan, where I graduated in 2006 with my BA. In the fall, I moved to NYC for an internship in fashion design.

After the internship I found a job in research, which was a support role for a fashion recruiter. I researched brands and their employees. Eventually I moved into recruiting in fashion and later, into tech recruiting. My husband works in animation and I was attracted to the LA weather, so we moved to sunny California. Through tech recruiting, I learned that web development seemed really fun, collaborative, and I was attracted to constant learning and problem-solving. Please enjoy my portfolio, and get in touch if you'd like!
